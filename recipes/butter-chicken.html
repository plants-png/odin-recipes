Butter Chicken

Description

This is a curry from India, that features tender, marinated chicken simmered in a rich, velvety and mildly spiced tomato-based sauce. It is known for its luxurious, creamy texture and aromaic flavour.

Ingredients

    Marinade

  • 1/2 cup plain yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp tumeric powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ginger, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 750 g chicken thigh fillets, cut into bite sized pieces

    • Curry

  • 2 tbsp ghee butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Marinade - Combine the Marinade ingredients with the chicken in a bowl. Cover and refrigerate overnight, or up to 24 hours (minimum 3 hours).
  2. Cook chicken - heat the ghee butter over high heat in a large frying pan. Take the chicken out of the marinade but do not wipe or shake off the marinade from the chicken. Don't pour the marinade left in the bowl into the frying pan.
  3. Place chicken in the frying pan and cook for around 3 minutes, or until the chicken is white all over.
  4. Sauce - add the tomato passata, cream, sugar and salt. Add any remaining marinade left in the bowl. Turn down to low and simmer for 20 minutes. Do a taste test to see if it needs more salt.
  5. Garnish with coriander/cilantro leaves if using. Serve with basmati rice.
